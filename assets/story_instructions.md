Larry needs as many apples as he can get. He's making apple pie to help the community.

Your Aunt Tina has a lot of apples. She's glad to trade some for some bananas. They don't cost much at first, but if everyone wants apples, then the price will go up.

You are a banana farmer. You make bananas to trade for apples. You can keep these apples, or trade them for banana tree seeds to make new trees. You grow your trees by answering questions. Whoever has the most apples at the end of the game wins.

Click on the tree to answer questions. In the questions, there are 2 buttons when you get a question correct. Continue and Close. Continue makes you go to the next question and close makes you leave.

When you want to trade, click on the Trading Post. The Trading Post is where you trade for apples and tree seeds.
